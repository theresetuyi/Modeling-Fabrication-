# Day 9. Final Fabrication, Documentation & Presentation
# Technical Drawings and Digital File Organization
Technical drawings are used to show the exact size, shape, and details of the final design. They help others understand how the project was made and how parts fit together.
All digital files such as CAD designs, STL files, and fabrication files were saved using clear names and organized in folders. Good file organization helps avoid confusion and makes it easy to update or reuse the design in the future.
# Reflection on Process, Materials, and Outcomes
This project helped me understand the full fabrication process from design to final result. I learned how to choose suitable materials, use different fabrication methods, and improve my design through corrections and adjustments.
Some challenges were faced during fabrication, but they helped me learn how to solve problems and work more carefully. Overall, the outcome was successful and improved my practical skills in digital fabrication.
